About bokeh.js

Bokeh is a platform for rich, interactive visualizations targeting the browser. bokeh.js is a front

The conceptual foundations of Bokeh originate from Leland Wilkinson's The Grammar of Graphics In this framework, all visualizations are built up from graphical primitives whose attributes can be vectorized over different data sources. The ggplot package is another system based on the Grammar of Graphics, for the R language.
